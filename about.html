About

I love reading debugging stories. This site is an attempt to read interesting stories on a regular basis.

{% endcomment %}

Contributing

Do you have an interesting story?

Whether you're working on video games, embedded software, webapps or enterprise software, I want to hear and publish it. Send me an email.

Interview format

I always ask the same questions to my guests:

  1. Who are you?
  2. What is the most interesting/funniest bug you've met?
  3. Anything else to add?

Apart from that, you've got carte blanche. It would help if you wrote your interview in Markdown though, but it's not mandatory.

Picture

I would also need a picture from you to illustrate the article. It has to be at least 550x550px. You don't have to figure on it if you don't want to.

Questions, remarks, contact

For anything else, don't hesitate to send me an email. I try my best to reply to every email.
